About Us

TILERSHUB is Sri Lanka’s leading platform for finding verified tilers and tile-related products. Our goal is to help homeowners find reliable professionals and affordable tools for any tiling project — big or small.

We aim to simplify the renovation process by providing a transparent, review-based system, smart calculators, and a trusted online directory of professionals.

Whether you are a homeowner or a professional tiler, TILERSHUB is designed to support your needs with accuracy, trust, and simplicity.
